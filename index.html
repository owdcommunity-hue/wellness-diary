My Story

Learning alongside you, not ahead of you.

I started Our Wellness Diary because I couldn't find a space that felt real. Every wellness account seemed polished, expert, and quietly intimidating.

So I decided to document everything — the wobbles, the discoveries, the diet experiments, the days I skipped practice. This channel is a diary, not a highlight reel.

My long-term goal is to become a certified yoga instructor and wellness coach. You're watching that journey happen in real time.
